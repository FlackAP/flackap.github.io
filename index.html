Who Is Flack AP?

My name is Andy Flack, and I'm a front end web developer. My story is a winding tale of seeking fortune in studies of history, art, and sustainable coffee. You can read more about that on my resume if you'd like, but the most important thing you should know about me is that I love working with people to solve problems, and that's why the world of programming is my place. I've never found a more welcoming community of people tenacious enough to tackle problems that will eventually effect almost every person on the planet.

Tools of the Trade

HTML5/CSS3

Using clean markup and stylesheets, I make it a point to write legible code that is easy to read and understand even if someone else comes in and touches my existing work.

JavaScript

Whether I'm animating some CSS, implementing server-side solutions with node, or utilizing an MVC-style framework for single-page webapps, Javascript is how the user experience comes to life.

Mobile-First Dev

Let's face it, there's a good chance you're looking at this on your mobile device at this very moment. In today's world, mobile-ready websites are a must in order to reach your clients effectively.
